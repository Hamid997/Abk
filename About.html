About Us

Abk Photography

I wasn't one of those people who got a camera at the age of five and fell in love with photography.

I was a freelance journalist, at the age of 22 when the magic of Marrakech begged me to take its photo.

Since that trip I became hooked on photographing patterns hidden within landscapes, extreme climates, cultures and the water world.

The Abk Photography works with clients to produce visual content and image/editing libraries for various publications and tourism/commercial clients, and also serves as a travel photography website.
